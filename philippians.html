Philippians: how living as a Christian means seeing our own
story as a living expression of Jesus' story. We are to imitate
His way of life. Even in our suffering, we can be content as we
mimic Him and draw closer to Him. While our true citizenship is
in heaven, we can enjoy a close connection to Jesus and an awareness
of His love and presence now that gives us hope in the darkest hours.
