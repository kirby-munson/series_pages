Philippians: how living as a Christian means seeing our own story as a living expression of Jesus' story. We are to imitate His way of life. Even in our suffering, we can be content as we mimic Him and draw closer to Him. While our true citizenship is in heaven, we can enjoy a close connection to Jesus and an awareness of His love and presence now that gives us hope in the darkest hours.

WEEK 7 QUESTIONS

1. Matt gave an update on “We Choose” and gave some details on Grace Church’s direction and plans for the future. What thoughts or questions do you have about this? How are you currently doing in the core value of Courageous Generosity?

2. When you feel distant or angry with God, what is most likely to pull your attention in another direction? What circumstances in your life are most likely to distance you from God? While you can’t always control the circumstances, what steps can you take to improve your response to them? What role can community play in this process?

3. Read Exodus 32 together as a group. What leadership lessons can we learn from the actions of both Moses and Aaron in this chapter? How could they each have handled things differently?

4. As the Christmas season approaches, what can your group do to best care for you and hold you accountable?
